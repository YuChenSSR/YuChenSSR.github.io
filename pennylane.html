pennylane

introduction

PennyLane is a QML (Quantum Machine Learning) framework introduced on September 22, 2023. It provides a comprehensive platform for quantum computing and machine learning integration. With PennyLane, users can develop and implement quantum algorithms, perform quantum simulations, and explore the potential of quantum machine learning. The framework is designed to be user-friendly and offers a range of tools and resources to support researchers and developers in their quantum computing endeavors.
